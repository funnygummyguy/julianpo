The Wonderful World of Potatoes

Potatoes have been around for centuries, and they’ve been part of human cuisine for longer than you might think. From crispy French fries to comforting mashed potatoes, they are the staple of many meals.

Did you know that there are over 4,000 different types of potatoes worldwide? Some potatoes are better for baking, others for frying, and some are perfect for boiling.

>

Potatoes are versatile, delicious, and an essential part of our culinary world. Next time you eat one, think of Julian and all the potatoes who never had a chance to fulfill their destiny!
